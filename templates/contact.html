Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. You'll find real-time updates on your delivery status. Alternatively, you can use the tracking link sent to your email after your order has been dispatched.
What are your delivery areas?
We currently deliver to all areas within the Central Region and parts of the Greater Accra Region in Ghana. We're continuously expanding our delivery zones. To check if we deliver to your area, enter your address during checkout or contact our customer service team.
How can I modify or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. Simply log into your account, go to "Order History," and select the order you wish to modify or cancel. If it's past the 2-hour window, please contact our customer service team directly for assistance.
What's your return policy for fresh produce?
We stand by the quality of our products. If you're not satisfied with the quality of any item you receive, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the items. We'll provide a refund or replacement for any items that don't meet our quality standards.
How do I join your weekly subscription box program?
You can sign up for our Weekly Veggie Box by visiting the "Subscription" page on our website. Choose your preferred box size, customize your selections if desired, and select your delivery frequency. You can pause, modify, or cancel your subscription at any time.
