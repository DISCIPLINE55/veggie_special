Our Story

Our Mission

At Veggie, we believe in reconnecting people with their food. Our mission is to create a sustainable food system by supporting local farmers and making fresh, organic produce accessible to everyone.

We strive to promote healthier eating habits while reducing the environmental impact of traditional grocery supply chains. By building direct relationships with farmers, we ensure fair prices for producers and the freshest products for our customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you select your partner farms?

We have a rigorous selection process that evaluates farming practices, produce quality, ethical labor practices, and environmental stewardship. We visit each farm personally before establishing a partnership and conduct regular follow-up visits.

What does "farm-to-table" actually mean for Veggie?

For us, farm-to-table means creating the shortest possible supply chain between farmers and consumers. Most produce is harvested within 24-48 hours of delivery to your door, preserving maximum freshness and nutritional value.

How do you ensure food safety?

All our partner farms follow strict food safety protocols and are regularly audited. Our distribution center maintains rigorous handling procedures, cold chain management, and quality control checks to ensure the safety of all products.

Are all your products organic?

While the majority of our products are certified organic, we also work with farms in transition to organic certification. These farms follow organic practices but may not yet have completed the certification process. All products are clearly labeled with their certification status.

How are you addressing climate change?

We address climate change through multiple initiatives: reducing food miles by sourcing locally, implementing energy-efficient operations, using electric delivery vehicles, offering compostable packaging, and investing in verified carbon offset projects for our remaining emissions.
